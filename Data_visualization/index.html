Consumer complaints against Navient Rise Sharply in 2024

By Tyler Quattrin
April 19, 2026

Consumer complaints involving Navient rose sharply in 2024, reaching 2,696, compared to 695 in 2023. Complaints then dropped to 732 in 2025 and 206 so far in 2026.

Most complaints focused on servicing problems. The largest category was “dealing with your lender or servicer” with 2,560 complaints, followed by 1,197 complaints about struggling to repay loans.

About 64.4% of complaints involved private student loans, compared to 35.6% for federal loans.

The data does not clearly support the claim that lawsuits are unsubstantiated, since borrower complaints remain frequent and focused on similar issues.
