The Minnesota Timberwolves are a professional basketball team based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The team competes in the Western Conference of the NBA and plays home games at Target Center.





The franchise became nationally relevant during the Kevin Garnett era in the late 1990s and early 2000s, including a Western Conference Finals appearance in 2004. Today, the team is led by superstar Anthony Edwards, who will soon bring the state of Minnesota a long-awaited championship with the support of new owners Marc Lore and A-Rod #WolvesBack.


The team’s current roster also includes stars Jaden McDaniels, Rudy Gobert, Julius Randle, Donte DiVincenzo, and Naz “Naz Reid” Reid.
