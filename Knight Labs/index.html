January 2025 Southern California wildfires

The 2025 Los Angeles wildfires burned thousands of acres across Southern California, forcing evacuations and damaging homes. Officials said dry conditions and strong winds helped the fires spread quickly. Fire crews worked to contain the blazes as residents fled affected areas. No official cause was confirmed, but investigators were looking into possible human and natural factors. The fires added to ongoing concerns about wildfire risk in the region.
